Who we are

Coyle’s Bakeshop specializes in handmade pastries, cakes, tarts, savories and sweets. We draw inspiration from classic French, English and American baking and strive to create exciting menus that showcase the best of every season here in the Pacific Northwest. We started in 2013 as a pop-up bakery and this spring, we’ll move into our brick & mortar shop at 8300 Greenwood Avenue North. For more information about our story, check out our Kickstarter page, or these write up in Seattle Weekly, Seattle Met and on Eater.com.

In addition to our baked goods, the café in Greenwood will serve espresso, light breakfast and lunch, and afternoon tea.

Thanks for stopping by. We can’t wait to see you at the shop very soon!
